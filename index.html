If you are already familiar with Zazen meditation, or just want to start meditating right away, feel free to use the meditation timer below. You can set the timer and the break to any number of minutes you wish. The recommended amount of time for beginners is 15 minutes, followed by 5 minutes of Kinhin, or walking meditation. Kinhin meditation consists of walking back-and-forth, or around the room with your eyes opened but relaxed (soft focus). Make a fist with your left hand, and cover the fist with your right hand. Place both of your hands under your diaphragm. Walk slowly, only taking the next step after the first is fully completed. Kinhin's idea is for you to keep your meditative state, but give your body a chance to move a bit and relax. Walk calmly, making your best effort to be present, and you will have done Kinhin.
